Pancakes

Description

This delicious looking stack of cakes is a breakfast favorite that you wont want to miss! You can make pancakes anytime of the day, after all breakfast can definitly be served for dinner!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Butter
  • Syrup

Steps

  1. In a large bowl mix 1 cup of flour with a 1/2 cup of water.
  2. Stir until contents are mixed evenly.
  3. Add a TBSP of butter on a large skillet and heat until butter has melted.
  4. Pour pancake mix into 3inch diameter circles and cook until they bubble.
  5. Once bubbles appear in the batter flip with a spatuala and cook or an additional minute.
  6. Stack your pancakes on a plate and add as much syrup as you want!
  7. Enjoy!!!
