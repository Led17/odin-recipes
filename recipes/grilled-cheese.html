Grilled Cheese

Description

This beer soaked grilled cheese is the perfect choice for lunch! Amber ale is used to give this classic sandwich a bit of a twist. Give it a try today and I'm sure you won't be disappointed! Check out all the ingredients and the steps below!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Butter
  • Beer
  • 2 Eggs
  • Milk

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl crack 2 eggs and add a 1/2 cup of milk and 1/2 cup of beer.
  2. Take two loaves of bread and soak each side in the contents of the bowl from Step 1.
  3. On a skillet place each slice with the desired amount of cheese slices.
  4. Fry up the slices for a few minutes and using a spatual place one slice on the other.
  5. While the Grilled Cheese is cooking finish downing the rest of the beer!
  6. Once both sides of the sandwich look equally finished cut in half and enjoy!
