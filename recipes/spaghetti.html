Spaghetti

Description

This bowl of spaghetti is cooked with the finest ingredients and can be made in under 30 minutes. The dish is easy enough to make even Dad's can do it! Your family will thank you as it quickly becomes a weekend tradition. Check out the quick and easy steps below along with the necessary ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Sauce
  • Olive Oil
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil water in a large pot.
  2. Once the water is at a rolling boil add a bit of olive oil and your desired amount of noodles.
  3. Let the noodles cook for 7-8 minutes
  4. In a seperate pan empty contents of your favorite sauce and cook on medium heat.
  5. Once your noodles are finished drain them from the water using a strainer.
  6. Palce desired amount of noodles in a bowl and add the sauce.
  7. Garnish with your favorite cheese and enjoy!
